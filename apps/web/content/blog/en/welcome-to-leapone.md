## Why this exists

Most small business owners don't know that AI search is now a thing. They know about Google. They might know about ChatGPT. What they don't know is that when their customer types "best plumber in Mississauga" into Perplexity, or asks Google AI Overview "where should I get my hair done in Halifax" — the answer comes from a small set of cited sources, and most Canadian small businesses are not in that set.

The tools that exist to fix this are mostly American. Their pricing assumes a US business buying for US directories. Their recommendations point at Yelp and BBB and skip HomeStars, TrustedPros, RateMDs, Realtor.ca, n49, and the rest of the Canadian directory landscape that actually matters here.

LeapOne is built specifically for Canadian SMBs and the AI search engines they need to show up in.

## What we audit

Three AI engines, in parallel, on every audit:

- **ChatGPT** (via OpenAI's `gpt-4o-mini` at temperature 0)
- **Perplexity** (via the `sonar` model — live web search)
- **Google AI Overview** (via SerpApi)

Plus the pieces that feed those engines: your Google Business Profile, your reviews, your website schema markup, the local pack, and now the directory citation surfaces — 28 directories tracked, including the Canadian-specific ones US tools ignore.

## What's different

Three things you won't find at our price tier ($19 Starter, $49 Pro):

1. **Vertical-specific Canadian directory recommendations.** Plumber? We surface HomeStars + TrustedPros. Dentist? RateMDs + Opencare. Lawyer? LawyerLocate + LinkedIn. Realtor? Realtor.ca via your CREA membership.
2. **Bilingual EN/FR**, including a Quebec-specific bilingual schema signal that tells Google's Knowledge Graph your entity serves both languages.
3. **Honest framing.** ChatGPT updates from training data, so it can take 6–12 months to reflect changes. We tell customers that out loud rather than promising what we can't deliver. Perplexity and Google AI Overview update much faster — that's where the actionable lift comes from in the first month.

## What's coming

We're shipping the AI execution coach next — a chat assistant attached to each recommendation that walks non-technical owners through actually doing the work. "Claim your HomeStars profile" becomes a guided 5-minute conversation, not a 200-word instruction you abandon.

That's the moat. SMB owners get told what to do; they rarely get walked through how to do it. We're building the part that's missing.
