Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 28, 2026

LeapOne ("we", "us", or "our") is operated by Marwa Saleh, based in Canada. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information in accordance with Canada's Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA).

Information We Collect

  • Name and email address (when you create an account)
  • Business name, type, and description (when you set up your profile)
  • Google and Facebook review data associated with your business (when you connect those accounts)

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the LeapOne service
  • To generate AI-drafted responses to your customer reviews using Anthropic's Claude AI
  • To display insights and analytics about your reviews
  • To communicate with you about your account

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate LeapOne:

  • Supabase (database and authentication) — supabase.com/privacy

  • Google (OAuth login and Business Profile API) — policies.google.com/privacy
  • Meta/Facebook (Facebook and Instagram API) — facebook.com/privacy/policy
  • Vercel (website hosting) — vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion at any time by contacting us at hello@leapone.ca.

Your Rights (PIPEDA)

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Withdraw consent and request deletion of your data
  • File a complaint with the Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada

Contact Us

For any privacy questions or data requests, contact us at hello@leapone.ca.
